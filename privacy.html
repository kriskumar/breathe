Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Soham is a breathing and meditation app made by an independent developer. The short version: the iOS app collects no data at all, and this website uses basic analytics. The details are below.

The Soham iOS app

The iOS app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No accounts. There is nothing to sign up for and no way to identify you.
  • No analytics, no ads, no trackers. The app contains no analytics or advertising SDKs and makes no tracking requests.
  • Everything stays on your device. Your settings and practice progress are stored locally on your iPhone and never leave it. We operate no servers and receive nothing from the app.
  • Heart-rate data is processed on-device only. If you connect a Bluetooth heart-rate monitor, readings are displayed live inside the app and are not stored by us or transmitted anywhere. Bluetooth access is used solely to talk to the monitor you choose.

Deleting the app deletes all of its data.

This website (pranaapp.org)

The web version of Soham at pranaapp.org uses Google Analytics to understand overall visitor numbers. Google Analytics may set cookies and collect usage data such as pages visited, approximate location, and device type, as described in Google's privacy policy. We do not collect names, emails, or any other personal information, and we do not sell or share data with anyone else.

Like the app, the web version keeps your settings and practice data in your browser's local storage — it never leaves your device.

Health disclaimer

Soham offers wellness guidance, not medical advice. Heart-rate readings are for personal awareness during practice and are not a medical measurement.

Children

Soham is not directed at children under 13 and collects no data from anyone, including children.
